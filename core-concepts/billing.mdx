## Understanding our different plans

ShipMagic offers 3 premium plans. The best plan for your store depends on which features you need.

#### Starter Plan

Best for stores using Shipping Customizations only. Choose the Starter Plan if you only need to:

- Use customizations in live mode
- Hide, rename, sort shipping rates

#### Pro Plan

Best for stores using Shipping Rates. Choose the Pro Plan if you need to:

- Use shipping rates in live mode
- Rates by zipcode, products, customer
- Live carrier rates

> Shipping Customizations are included in this plan as well.

#### Pro\+ Plan

Best for highly complex shipping requirements. Choose the Pro\+ Plan if your shipping logic cannot be achieved using the standard rule engine.

With Pro\+, we offer custom-coded shipping logic tailored to your business needs.

## How to cancel subscription?

If you wish to cancel your subscription, simply uninstall the app from store. Your subscription is canceled automatically. App removal is fully clean with no residual code left behind. 

Your rules and settings are preserved. If you reinstall the app in the future, you can reactivate a plan and continue using your saved setup.
