## What is ShipMagic?

ShipMagic is a powerful shipping rate calculator for Shopify that lets you create flexible, rule-based shipping logic to deliver accurate and personalized rates at checkout. This helps you:

- Stop losing money on shipping
- Boost your checkout conversion rate
