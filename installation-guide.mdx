Follow these steps to install and connect ShipMagic with your Shopify store.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Shopify App Store">
    Install ShipMagic  from [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/shipping-calculator-shipmagic?referrer=mintlify-installation-guide).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Shipping Rates Installation ">
    Navigate to Settings → Shipping and Delivery → General Profile.

    If ShipMagic appears as a shipping rate provider in your zones, the app is set up and connected correctly.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 04 25 At 6 30 15 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-04-25-at-6.30.15-PM.png)
    </Frame>

    <Note>
      If you don’t see ShipMagic App listed in your shipping zones, check the following:

      #### 1. Verify you are checking in the General Shipping Profile

      By default, ShipMagic connects to the General shipping profile.

      If you’re using a custom profile, you’ll need to connect the app manually - follow the setup steps here.

      #### 2. Ensure CCS is enabled on your store

      Carrier Calculated Shipping (CCS) is a [Shopify feature](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/fulfillment/setup/shipping-rates/third-party-carrier-calculated-shipping) that allows third-party apps like ShipMagic to display shipping rates at checkout.

      This is mandatory and is required for all third party shipping rate apps.\
      \
      You can contact [Shopify customer support](https://help.shopify.com/en#/contact) in order to enable CCS for your store.
    </Note>

    <Info>
      **Tip:** If you're unsure whether CCS is enabled, go to the **Shipping Rates** page in the app.

      If CCS is not enabled, you’ll see an error message. If no error is shown, CCS is active on your store.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Shipping Customizations Installation">
    Shipping Customizations are installed automatically and do not require any manual verification.

    They are enabled by default and work seamlessly across all stores.
  </Step>
</Steps>
